Membership

Join the coolest historical society of them all!

Becoming an official member of the NJ Public Auto Museum is a fun way to get involved in the car community and to support the museum's mission.

There are 2 membership types: one free, and one paid. Either way, when you become a member, you help the museum grow! Sign up now!

Standard Membership

It's free!

  • Receive our quarterly newsletter.
  • Be the first to know when new vehicles arrive.

Heritage Membership

For just $50 a year, you can be a Heritage Member of the NJ Public Auto Museum! Choose auto-renewal at checkout, and get 30% off!

  • Exclusive access to the museum outside normal hours. During this special members-only time, you'll have the museum entirely to yourself – plus your friendly museum host, of course! Please make an appointment to take advantage of this membership benefit.
  • An official certificate commemorating your first joining the museum.
  • A special “Gift of the Year,” such as a museum-branded mug or classic car poster.
