Do you love CLASSIC CARS?

Then come visit us at the Vintage Automobile Museum of New Jersey, where we love classic autos as much as you do!

Our mission is to preserve and spread the word about the greatest automotive history from all across the world — but especially from here at home in NJ, USA.

Admission is always FREE!

Here's how YOU can be part
of automotive history TODAY!

Visit!

As soon as you step in, HISTORY COMES TO LIFE before your very eyes!

Our vintage automobiles are carefully maintained and stored by museum members from all across the state and displayed in the museum on a semi-monthly rotation.

Come back often to see the latest displays!

Contribute!

Being a nonprofit organization, we rely completely on your generous patronage and deeply appreciate donations of all kinds.

One incredibly rewarding way to help out is to buy tickets for our ANNUAL RAFFLE – and possibly drive away in this year’s classic auto!

Volunteering is another great way to help the museum achieve its mission. Join our AMAZING STAFF, and do your part to keep our shared automotive history alive!

Contact us today to see how you can get involved!
