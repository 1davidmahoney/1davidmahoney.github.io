Every little bit helps

Our museum is free to the public because we believe automotive history belongs to everyone. We love classic cars, and we're thrilled to provide a space where the community can share that passion together. It's been a dream come true that we hope to continue for decades to come!

Although government grants help us stay afloat, we can't achieve our biggest dreams for the museum without additional funding.

By donating, you're helping us reach our true potential. Whether you're a certified “car nut” or simply want to contribute, your donation today means we can do more than just keep the lights on... Together, we can put spotlights on even more classic cars!

Funding goals

We're proud to have served the community from our current location for many years, but we haven't yet achieved our true vision for the museum.

From our humble beginnings as a mobile car display, to now having a building in beautiful Point Pleasant, NJ, we're just getting started!

When you donate, you help us achieve...

  • Amazing classic cars
  • “Museum on Wheels”
  • Physical location
  • Larger building with more parking
  • More amazing classic cars and automotive memorabilia
  • Interactive digital displays
  • Classic car driving simulator

...and other awesome things we haven't even dreamt of yet!

Got ideas of your own? Let us know!
