Hello there!

Thanks for visiting! I hope you enjoy my portfolio as much as I've enjoyed compiling it.

For over a decade, I've made a career mixing two of my favorite activities: sharing a good story, and playing with computers. Being a narrative-focused game designer means I have a unique sense of empowerment wherein I'm able to dream up exciting, emotional story moments and then go a step further by bringing them to life in game engines.

I've worked with proprietary tools (Telltale; RAD) and industry-standard tools (Unreal; Maya) on content ranging from original IP (Lone Echo II) to franchises beloved “across the galaxy...” The scope of work I've done has ranged from mobile QA to technical narrative design, from writing dialogue to prototyping new modes of interaction in VR.

I'm always open to talk shop, so if you'd like to chat, please feel free to reach out! Check out my résumé here!
