Hello there!

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! I hope you enjoy viewing each piece as much as I've enjoyed making it.

For over a decade, I've been proud and grateful to make a career mixing two of my favorite activities: sharing a good story, and playing with computers. Exploring these pastimes has led to a unique sense of empowerment, wherein I'm able to dream up exciting, emotional narratives and then help make them happen via professional development tools.

I've worked with proprietary tools (Telltale; RAD) and industry-standard tools (Unreal; Maya) on content ranging from original IP to franchises beloved across "the galaxy..." The scope of work I've done has ranged from mobile QA to VR technical narrative design, from writing dialogue to prototyping new modes of interaction.

I'm always open to talk shop, so if you'd like to chat, please feel free to reach out! Check out my résumé here!
