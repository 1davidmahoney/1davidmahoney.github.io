Visiting the Museum

We are located a short drive from the Garden State Parkway, near Route 35.

Our museum is wheelchair-accessible, and we are committed to ensuring all visitors have a comfortable experience. Please let us know if you have any special needs or require additional accommodations.

Vehicle Display

Different vehicles are brought in every 2-3 months, allowing you to learn about all eras of automotive history.

Sometimes, there will be special exhibitions that disrupt the regularly scheduled rotation of vehicles. For example, we might randomly host an all-British car display for a month.

Visit often to see all the amazing vehicles the museum has to offer!

Gift Shop
