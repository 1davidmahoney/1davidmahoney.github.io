Gallery

The museum has housed countless vehicles over the years, ranging from the groundbreaking Model T’s of the early 20th century to the muscle cars of the '60s and '70s. Vehicle owners include the museum organization, the museum's trustees, donors, and local car clubs.

Hosting and attending events is also a big part of what we do at VAMNJ. Browse the gallery below, and see all the fun we've had over the years!

This is just a glimpse of what the museum has to offer. To get the full experience, plan your visit today!

Current Display: Cars of the Century

These vehicles come from all across automotive history. From hand-cranked roadsters to the muscle car era, this collection shows how vastly vehicle design has changed over the years.

Fall 2024 – Museum Display – The Roaring ‘20s

The magic and mystery of the 1920s come to life! Always a fan favorite, this collection represents an elevated style and attention to detail not found in any other era of car production.

Winter 2023 – Car Show – New York City

The NYC Car Show is a longtime favorite event for the museum to attend, as thousands of automotive enthusiasts from all around the world will visit. It's the perfect opportunity to let everyone know about the museum!

Spring 2022 – Car Show – Allaire State Park

Museum board members attended the annual car show at Allair State Park, spreading the word about the museum and even displaying a few cars of our own!

Summer 2021 – Museum Display – British Invasion!

The museum was incredibly excited to partner with the British Motor Club in hosting their wonderful vehicles from “across the pond.” The display was so popular, we had to keep it going the entire summer!
