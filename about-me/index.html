Hello there!

Thanks for visiting! I hope you enjoy my portfolio as much as I've enjoyed compiling it.

For over a decade, I've made a career mixing two of my favorite activities: sharing a good story, and playing with computers. Being a narrative-focused game designer has helped me develop a wonderful sense of empowerment wherein I'm able to dream up exciting, emotionally fulfilling gameplay moments and then go a step further by bringing them to life in cutting-edge game engines.

I've worked with proprietary tools (at Telltale; Ready At Dawn) and industry-standard tools (Unreal; Maya) on content ranging from original IP (Lone Echo II) to franchises beloved across the world (Guardians of the Galaxy; Game of Thrones; one other “galactically” popular IP, but only during pre-production).

My professional journey has taken me from testing games and taking notes during writers' rooms to writing dialogue for a dramatic space adventure game; to prototyping new modes of character interaction in VR; and ultimately to collaborating with studio leadership on the development of new original IP.

I'm always open to talk shop, so if you'd like to chat, please feel free to reach out! Check out my résumé here!
