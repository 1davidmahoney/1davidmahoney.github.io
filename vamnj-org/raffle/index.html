Annual 50-50 Raffle

One of the major ways the museum funds its operations is a yearly raffle. This year, we're trying something new!

We're selling 2000 raffle tickets at $50 apiece. With this format, we hope to appeal to the widest possible audience, so that we might achieve our wildest dreams for the museum.

How it works

Drawing and payout

A single winner will be chosen at the drawing, which will occur on November 8, 2025. Winnings are 50% of the total pot.

  • Grand Prize: $50,000
    • (Maximum payout, if pot is full)

The remainder will help fund museum operations and support our long-term goals.

Ticket sales

Tickets for the next drawing will be sold from January to November 2025. To purchase tickets, please contact us.
