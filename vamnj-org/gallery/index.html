Gallery

The museum has housed countless vehicles over the years, ranging from the groundbreaking Model T’s of the early 20th century to the muscle cars of the '60s and '70s. Vehicle owners include the museum organization, the museum's trustees, donors, and local car clubs.

Hosting and attending events is also a big part of what we do at VAMNJ. Browse the gallery below, and see all the fun we've had over the years!

This is just a glimpse of what the museum has to offer. To get the full experience, plan your visit today!

Currently at the Museum: Pre-war Cars

Discover the innovation and style of the pre-war era with this collection of classic cars from the 1920s and 1930s—a glimpse into the vehicles that set the standard before World War II changed the automotive world forever.

Museum Display: Pick-up Trucks

Imagine working in such style! Although aesthetics have changed, the classic form of the pick-up truck persists to this very day.

Museum Display: Stock Cars

The Garden State Stock Car Club was kind enough to display some of their members' amazing road-tested vehicles at the museum. Feel the need—the need for SPEED!

Museum Display: Cars of the 50s & 60s

Celebrate the golden age of motoring with bold designs and timeless icons that defined two unforgettable decades of automotive history.
