Donations

Every little bit helps

Our greatest wish is for the museum to continue operating for decades to come, so future generations can continue enjoying the automotive legacy passed down to us.

Although grants and raffles help us stay afloat, we can't achieve our biggest dreams for the museum without additional funding.

By donating, you're becoming part of our shared automotive history and helping us reach our true potential. Whether you're a certified “car nut” or simply want to contribute, your donation today means we can do more than just keep the lights on... Together, we can put spotlights on even more classic cars!

Types of Donations

We deeply appreciate donations of all kinds, including the examples below. Whatever method of donation you choose, our financial coordinators are available to guide you through the process, including finding reputable appraisers.

  • Money (see form below)
  • Automotive memorabilia
  • Classic vehicles
  • Planned giving
  • Legacy giving

For vehicle donations in particular, we understand how difficult it can be to part ways. These classic cars remind us of times shared with loved ones or simply a life well lived. Whatever your reason for donating, you can rest assured your vehicle will have a good home at the museum, where it will continue to wow guests for years to come.

Funding goals

We're proud to have served the community from our current location for many years, but we haven't yet achieved our true vision for the museum.

From our humble beginnings as a mobile car display, to now having a building in beautiful Point Pleasant, NJ, we're just getting started!

When you donate, you help us achieve...

  • Amazing classic cars
  • “Museum on wheels” events
  • Physical location
  • Larger building with more parking
  • More amazing classic cars and automotive memorabilia
  • Interactive digital displays
  • Classic car driving simulator

...and other awesome things we haven't even dreamt of yet!

Got ideas of your own? Let us know!
