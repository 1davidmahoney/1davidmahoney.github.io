Funding idea

  1. Start a GoFundMe account.
  2. Whenever we have a major project we'd like help funding, use GoFundMe to run a fundraiser campaign.
  3. Link to the campaign from our list of “funding goals and dream projects,” mention it on social media, and just generally tell everyone we know.
  4. Update the list as we go, crossing out goals as they're achieved and adding special notes for ones that were crowd-funded.

For example:

  • Start a campaign to raise $10,000 for a nice classic car.
  • Keep it as a permanent “photo op” vehicle for visitors to take pictures in.
  • On our web page, mark the goal as “successfully crowd-funded,” so people can see that they really can make a difference by donating.
