Hop-In Photo Op

Ever wonder what it would feel like to sit inside a classic car? Now's your chance!

Get behind the steering wheel, and have your photo taken in style!

How it works

  1. Find your friendly museum host.
  2. Take a seat in the specially marked classic car.
  3. Strike a cool pose!
  4. Take some selfies, or ask the host to take some pictures with your phone.

Price: FREE!

We kindly ask that you please be careful in and around the vehicle, to avoid damaging it. Please note that we may not be able to accommodate all visitors due to design constraints of the past.
