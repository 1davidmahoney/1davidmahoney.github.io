Membership

Join the coolest historical society of them all!

Becoming an official member of the Vintage Automobile Museum of NJ is a fun way to get involved in the car community and to support the museum's mission.

Not ready to become a member? Be a Friend of the Museum! Either way, when you sign up below, you help the museum grow!

Friends of the Museum

It's free!

  • Receive our quarterly newsletter.
  • Be the first to know when new vehicles arrive.

Members

For just $50 a year, you can be a full-fledged Member of the Vintage Automobile Museum of NJ! Choose auto-renewal at checkout, and get 30% off!

When you become a VAMNJ member, you'll receive...

  • An official certificate commemorating your first joining the museum.
  • A special “Gift of the Year,” such as a museum-branded mug or classic car poster.
  • A 25% discount at our gift shop.
  • A 15% discount on our venue rental fee.

Volunteer Opportunities

Being a nonprofit organization, everything we do depends on volunteers, so we deeply appreciate your interest in helping our cause.

The easiest way to volunteer at VAMNJ is to become a standard member (see form below). Then, stay tuned for announcements about volunteer opportunities.
