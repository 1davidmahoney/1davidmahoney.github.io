Membership

Join the coolest historical society of them all!

Becoming an official member of the Vintage Automobile Museum of NJ is a fun way to get involved in the car community and to support the museum.

Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteering with the museum gives you a chance to give back to the community—all while hanging out around awesome classic cars! We welcome people of all ages and experience levels to join us in our mission to promote, preserve, and protect vintage automobiles.

Museum membership is not required in order to volunteer.

Being a nonprofit organization, everything we do depends on the noble spirit of volunteerism, so we deeply appreciate your interest in helping our cause. Contact us today to discuss ways you can help.

Become a Member or Sponsor

Support the preservation and celebration of automotive history by becoming a member or sponsor of the Vintage Automobile Museum of NJ. Your membership helps fund exhibits, events, and educational programs, while offering you special benefits and the satisfaction of supporting a nonprofit community organization. Sponsorships are also available for individuals and businesses who wish to make an even greater impact.

Simply fill out this form and mail along with your payment to the indicated address.

When you become a VAMNJ member or sponsor, you'll receive...

  • An engraved plaque commemorating your first joining the museum.
  • Special access to exclusive members-only events.
  • A reserved seat at our monthly meeting.
