A knack for the cinematic

Because of technology like Unreal Engine 5, video games have become more cinematic than ever: They transition between passive and interactive entertainment so smoothly you almost forget you're playing a game. Environments come to life like never before, and character performance is now so natural that you can't resist being moved by it.

The portfolio piece below is mainly meant to show my penchant for the cinematic and my proficiency with modern tools like Unreal Engine 5. This piece is also meant to demonstrate my Narrative Design skills in action, by way of the creative sensibilities used to plan the piece and the technical know-how used to implement it.

Game Specs

  • Title: Runt
  • Audience: Young adults and up (rated T for Teen)
  • Genre: Action-adventure
  • Platform: Console/PC
  • Style: 3D; third-person; semi-gritty with bright spots.
  • Logline: The runaway runt of a family of alien settlers must uncover his hidden superpowers in order to save everyone from an evil mountain hermit.
  • Message: It's okay if you change in the process of finding yourself, because family will always be at your side.

Game Overview

Runt is a fable-like underdog adventure story, where the hero essentially goes from sad puppy to brave guard dog.

You play as the youngest and weakest brother in a big family of superhuman-esque beings on a verdant alien homestead, where survival means being able to fight all kinds of vicious creatures.

One day, your siblings' teasing goes too far, so you run away from home in search of self-worth and purpose. You cross paths with an evil hermit and realize you must become more powerful than anyone ever imagined in order to save your family and home.

Thankfully, with the help of your trusty pet and a few friends you make along the way, you're able to overcome your fear and self-doubt and discover the strength within yourself to save the day. Despite how much you've changed, you and your family are just happy to have you back home again.

Level 1 Overview

This portfolio piece zooms in on one particular level within the hypothetical Runt game: the very start, which is meant to include a higher concentration of cinematic content in order to draw the player into the rest of the game.

  • Plot: You (Thorn) help your giant siblings prepare for the morning hunt.
  • Character Story: You get teased a lot for bing the runt of the family, but it's never truly malicious, so everyone seems to get along well enough, even though you can't help but feel hurt by it after a while.
  • Implied Author: “If there's one thing to know about Thorn, it's that what he lacks in size he makes up for in resolve and nobility.”
